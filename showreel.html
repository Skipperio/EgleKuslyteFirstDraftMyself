Showreel

Hi, I’m Egle Kuslyte, filmmaker, blogger and a philosopher.

My first ever movie LIGHT VS DARK. Poetic documentary.

It was my idea, I directed it and edited it. I had some help from 2 people to hold my camera and play with lights, but otherwise all movie was made by me.

I participated in project – Transnational Filmmaking: Recording of Cultural Identities Through Oral Histories. With my team we created movie – SCAVENGER DREAM. In SDG Filmfest we won two awards: DOCUMENTARY MEDIA CENTRE SHOWCASE AWARD and #DMUGLOBAL ORAL HISTORIES AWARD. EATSA ARTLAB festival selected our short-film.
