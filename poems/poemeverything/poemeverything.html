POEM – Everything
When you finally have nothing, you understand that you actually have everything. You have to lose it in order to find it.

  • I really love our time - the present.
  • When the word - love - is not real.
  • I really love that there is no suffer.
  • Same with it everything was lost.

  • To see human and love him for what he is.
  • That is my vulnerability and charm.
  • You don’t need to win my heart.
  • Because I don’t have it anymore.

  • My heart belongs to no one.
  • Broken and fallen. Real and honest.
  • I accept those who wishes my help.
  • Same I get them from a dream world.

  • No one needs to prove me something.
  • That there is something or isn’t.
  • The world is and I’m happy.
  • Meaning is in our simple being.

  • That’s when eye opens. Sees everything.
  • That’s when secret opens to destroy you.
