POEM – Bridges

  • Two humans in different sides of shore.
  • Black van is riding – it is amazing.
  • Hides behind the dark windows.
  • Human deep feelings are hidden.

  • Bridges unite cities walls.
  • Bridges are in our veins.
  • Road which seems lonely.
  • Further always split into parts.

  • It lets you to decide.
  • Accept or come back.
  • It lets you to understand.
  • Agree or reject.
