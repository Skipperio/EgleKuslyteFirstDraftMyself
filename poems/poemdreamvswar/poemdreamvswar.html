POEM – Dream vs War

  • I will wake up,
  • But I don’t want to.
  • My real world is a dream.
  • Peace, love and hope in there.

  • I feel empty in reality.
  • I don’t want this!
  • This is what I wish for!
  • War is in reality.

  • Dream is in a dream.
