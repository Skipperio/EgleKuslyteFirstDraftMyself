  • Listen for that wind.
  • Which was louder before.
  • Listen for that grass.
  • Which was growing before.

  • Everything was demolished.
  • Even the smallest ones.
  • Nothing is here anymore.
  • Even the biggest ones.

  • Where everybody disappeared?
  • Who destroyed them?
  • Yes, this is OUR fault.
  • We didn't appreciate the value.
