  • I can see you in the park.
  • In the road, crossroad, near the bench.
  • I can see you in the stairway.
  • In home, on the sofa near computer.

  • I can't see you outside.
  • In graveyard, sitting near the tree.
  • I can't see you in my heart.
  • Your soul left me.

  • I only see your eyes.
  • Which watches how I die.

Andrej Kralovic
